Academic Note on PRESCRIPTION_MEDICATION (M:N bridge): This is the correct pattern for the M:N between PRESCRIPTION and MEDICATION. The composite PK ensures no duplicate medication in the same prescription. The extra attributes (times_per_day, dose, directions, start_date, end_date) live here because they describe the relationship between a specific prescription and a specific medication — not either entity alone.

Academic Note on CONTACT_FORM: The nullable FK to USER_ACCOUNT allows both authenticated users and anonymous visitors to submit enquiries. This is modelled as a partial participation (zero-or-one) on the USER_ACCOUNT side.
